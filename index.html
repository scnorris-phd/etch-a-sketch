Etch-A-Sketch

by Steven Norris for the Odin Project's Foundations course

Instructions:
  • The Etch-A-Sketch defaults to a 16x16 black and white grid.
  • To fill a cell, hold down the "Control" button on your keyboard and hover your pointer over the cell.
  • Click on the "New Grid" button to generate a new custom-sized black and white grid.
  • Click the "Color Toggle" to make the Etch-A-Sketch fabulous!
